Rentals

Winding through canyons in central Utah, in some of the most desolate land anywhere, the Green river flows towards the mighty Colorado river and the sea. High grey stone cliffs, beautiful desert scenery, and great rapids have made this run famous. This 84-mile trip runs from from Sand Wash to Swaseys Rapid (just outside Green River Utah) and boasts outstanding scenery, interesting geologic formations, evidence of prehistoric and historic human activity, and great whitewater opportunities.

Desolation is rated a Class II/III and is usually done in 6 days

Desolation Canyon has been recognized as a National Landmark. This designation is based on the uniqueness and rich history that the canyon tells.

Embark on a heart-pounding adventure with Banos Rapids in Ecuador! Nestled in the stunning Andes, our white-water rafting experience offers thrills for both beginners and experts. Conquer rapids surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, guided by experts ensuring safety and excitement. Join us for an extraordinary journey that blends adrenaline and nature, creating unforgettable memories in the wild beauty of Banos, Ecuador.

Banos, Ecuador, nestled in the Andes at the base of Tungurahua volcano, boasts a stunning natural landscape with lush greenery, hot springs, and waterfalls. Surrounded by mountains and bordered by the Pastaza River, Banos offers a mild climate and serves as a perfect gateway for outdoor activities. Its unique geography makes it a haven for nature lovers and adventurers, showcasing the diverse beauty of Ecuador's landscapes.

CLASS I - X: Banos, Ecuador's rapids are often classified in the range of Class II to Class IV, offering a mix of moderate to challenging whitewater experiences.
